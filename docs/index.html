Privacy Policy for Platterlytics

Last updated: February 05, 2026

Summary: Platterlytics is an offline-first application. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any external servers.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Platterlytics. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we handle your personal data when you use our mobile application.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Platterlytics operates as a standalone, offline-first application. This means:

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • No Usage Tracking: We do not track how you use the application.
  • No External Transmission: All data you enter into the app (such as menu items, bills, and sales history) is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this data to any cloud servers or third parties.

3. Data Storage

All application data is stored locally on your device using:

  • SQLite Database: For structured data like menu items and bills.
  • Shared Preferences: For app settings and preferences.

If you uninstall the application or clear its data, this locally stored information will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered by us.

4. Permissions

The application may request the following permissions for specific functionalities:

  • Internet: While the app is offline-first, standard internet permissions may be present for basic system functions or future updates, but no user data is sent.
  • Storage / File Access: Required only if you choose to export bills or reports (e.g., as PDF or images) to save them to your device.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising services that track users.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
