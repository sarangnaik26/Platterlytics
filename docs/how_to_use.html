Guide: How to Use Platterlytics

Platterlytics is a powerful, offline-first tool designed to help you manage your business sales and analyze performance with ease. This guide will help you get the most out of the app.

1. Setting Up Your Menu

Before you start billing, you need to add your items to the menu.

  • Go to the Menu tab.
  • Tap the + button to add a new category or item.
  • Define the Item Name and Price. You can also add multiple units (e.g., Half, Full) for the same item.
  • Use the Edit icon to update prices or item details at any time.

2. Managing Bills

Creating bills is the core of the app. It's designed for speed.

  • From the Home screen, tap the Add Bill button.
  • Select items from your menu. Tapping an item adds it to the current bill.
  • Adjust quantities using the + and - buttons in the bill preview.
  • Tap Save & Print (or Save) to record the transaction.
Pro Tip: You can search for items by name or category to quickly build large orders.

3. Understanding Analytics

The Analytics tab provides deep insights into your sales patterns.

Daily & Range Analysis

View your total sales, bill counts, and hourly trends for any specific day or date range.

Weekday Analysis (Smart Logic)

This section analyzes performance based on the day of the week (e.g., how do my Mondays perform?).

  • Growth Rate: Compares performance against the same day last week. It is based on Sales Amount (Money) to show financial growth.
  • Consistency: Tells you if a day is "Stable" (predictable sales) or "Variable" (high fluctuations).
  • Daily Contribution: Shows what percentage of your total weekly revenue comes from that specific day.
  • Peak Day: Identifies which day of the week brings in the most revenue on average.
The "Monday Switch" Logic: To ensure your data is accurate, the weekday analysis only includes completed weeks.
  • The data will only update to include last week's performance once **Monday morning** arrives.
  • This prevents mid-week days (like a Wednesday morning) from showing low averages just because the week hasn't finished yet.

4. Customizing Settings

Tailor the app to your business needs in the Settings tab.

  • Appearance: Switch between Light, Dark, or System themes.
  • Bill Settings: Add your business name, address, and contact info to appear on generated bills. You can also customize the currency symbol.
  • Data Management:
    • Auto-Delete: Set the app to automatically clear bills older than 3, 6, or 12 months to save space.
    • Cache Clearing: Keep the app running fast by clearing temporary files.
